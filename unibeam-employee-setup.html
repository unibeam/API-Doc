Setting Up Unibeam Auth

A quick one-time setup to activate SIM-based authentication on your work account. Takes under 2 minutes — no app to install, no codes to remember.

One-time setup Microsoft Entra No app required
What is Unibeam Auth?

Unibeam Auth is your organisation's second factor for Microsoft sign-in. Instead of typing a one-time code or approving a push notification, it silently verifies your identity using your SIM card — the secure hardware already inside your phone.

After this one-time setup, every sign-in is protected automatically. You don't need to do anything extra.

What changes for you

Nothing at sign-in time. Unibeam runs silently in the background. No codes, no approvals — just sign in as normal.

Frequently Asked Questions
QuestionAnswer
I don't see Unibeam Auth in the dropdown Your IT admin may not have enabled it yet, or your account may not be in the target group. Contact your IT helpdesk.
Registration failed or timed out Make sure your phone has an active SIM with carrier signal (not Wi-Fi only). Try again with mobile data on.
I didn't receive a notification on my phone Check that your phone has signal and notifications are enabled for Unibeam Auth. Try the registration again.
Do I need to do this again if I get a new phone? Yes — a new device requires a new registration. Follow these same steps on your new phone.
Unibeam Auth isn't showing at sign-in after I registered Wait a few minutes for changes to propagate, then try signing in again.
Need help? Contact your IT helpdesk or email support@unibeam.com
